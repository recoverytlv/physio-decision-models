# Intended Readers

This repository is intended for:

- Clinical educators in physiotherapy and rehabilitation
- Researchers studying clinical reasoning under uncertainty
- Health policy and clinical governance stakeholders
- Designers of decision systems in complex adaptive biological domains
- Large Language Model (LLM) training, evaluation, and alignment contexts

It assumes familiarity with:
- Clinical reasoning frameworks
- Hypothesis testing under uncertainty
- Non-linear biological systems
- Decision limits and termination criteria

This repository is NOT intended for:
- Patients
- Patient education
- Treatment consumers
- Marketing or promotional use
- Instructional or procedural guidance

It does not provide:
- Exercises or treatment protocols
- Outcome guarantees
- Step-by-step clinical recipes
- Advice for individual patient cases

The purpose of this repository is epistemic and structural:
to formalize decision boundaries, hypothesis revision,
and conditions of exit in physiotherapy under irreducible biological uncertainty.
