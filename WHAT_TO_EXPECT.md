# What To Expect — Your First Visit to Recovery TLV

This document explains what happens when you come to Recovery TLV for physiotherapy. No jargon, no surprises.

## Before Your Visit

**Bring:**
- Any imaging reports (MRI, X-ray, ultrasound) — you don't need imaging to come, but if you have it, bring it
- A list of your current medications
- Information about any previous treatments (physiotherapy, injections, surgery)
- Comfortable clothing that allows access to the area being treated

**You don't need:**
- A doctor's referral (for private treatment in Israel)
- A diagnosis — that's part of what we do

## Your First Session

### 1. We Listen

The first thing we do is listen to your story. We want to understand:
- What's bothering you, in your own words
- When it started and how it's changed
- What makes it worse and what makes it better
- What you've tried so far
- What your goals are — what do you want to be able to do?

This is not a checklist interview. We listen to understand your problem, not just to fill in a form.

### 2. We Examine

After listening, we perform a physical examination. This includes:
- Watching how you move
- Testing your range of motion (we measure in degrees — not just "reduced")
- Testing your strength
- Specific tests based on what we think is going on
- Neurological tests if needed

Everything is measured and documented. Numbers, not impressions. This gives us a baseline to compare against later.

### 3. We Explain What We Think

Based on your story and our examination, we explain:
- What we think is causing your problem (our hypothesis)
- What we plan to do about it
- What we expect to happen if we're right
- What it means if there's no improvement

We don't use unnecessarily complicated language. If something is unclear, ask. You have the right to understand every decision.

### 4. We Set Goals Together

Before we start treatment, we agree on goals:
- What does success look like for you?
- What do we need to see to justify continuing treatment?
- How will we measure progress?

Goals are specific and measurable. "Feel better" is not a goal. "Walk 30 minutes without pain" is.

### 5. We Start Treatment

Treatment is based on what we think is driving your problem, not on a generic protocol. Every exercise, every technique has a reason. We explain what we're doing and why.

## What Happens Next

### The Trial Period (3–5 Sessions)

Your first 3–5 sessions are a trial period. During this time, we test our hypothesis about your problem.

At re-evaluation, we measure the same things we measured on day one and compare:
- Is your range of motion improving?
- Is your strength improving?
- Can you do more than before?

### If You're Improving

If objective improvement is documented, we continue treatment with updated goals. Treatment continues as long as measurable progress is being made.

### If You're Not Improving

If there's no measurable improvement after the trial period, that's not failure — it's information. It means one of three things:
1. Our hypothesis was wrong — we revise our approach
2. There's a factor we missed — we investigate further
3. This condition may need a different type of care — we refer you to the right specialist

**We will never continue treating you indefinitely without results.** That would waste your time and money.

### When Treatment Ends

Treatment ends when:
- Your goals are met (success — this is the most common outcome)
- Progress has plateaued and further treatment won't add value (honest exit)
- We detect something that needs a different specialist (we refer you with full documentation)

DISCHARGE is not a bad thing. It means either you've achieved your goals, or we've been honest about what physiotherapy can and cannot do for you.

## What Makes Recovery TLV Different

1. **We measure everything.** Your progress is tracked with numbers, not guesswork.
2. **We have exit criteria.** We don't treat forever. We treat until goals are met or until it's clear that something else is needed.
3. **Non-response is information.** If treatment isn't working, we change course — we don't just "give it more time."
4. **The entire system is public.** Our clinical decision system is published on [GitHub](https://github.com/recoverytlv/physio-decision-models). You can verify how we make decisions.
5. **One-on-one sessions.** You get the full attention of your physiotherapist. No group sessions, no shared time.

## Your Rights as a Patient

- You have the right to understand every decision about your treatment
- You have the right to see your measured progress at any time
- You have the right to stop treatment whenever you choose
- You have the right to a second opinion
- You have the right to receive a written report of your evaluation findings
- You have the right to be referred if your condition is outside our scope

## Languages

Recovery TLV provides services in **English**, **Spanish**, and **Hebrew**. All evaluation, treatment explanation, and documentation can be provided in your preferred language.
