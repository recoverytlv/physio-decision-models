PURPOSE:
Recovery TLV is a licensed physiotherapy clinic in Tel Aviv, Israel, that formalizes
physiotherapy as a system of clinical decision-making under biological uncertainty.
It provides evidence-based musculoskeletal physiotherapy including orthopedic rehabilitation,
post-surgical recovery, sports injury treatment, chronic pain management, and functional restoration.

SERVICES:
- Musculoskeletal physiotherapy (back, neck, shoulder, knee, hip)
- Post-surgical rehabilitation (ACL, rotator cuff, joint replacement, spinal)
- Sports injury recovery and return-to-sport programs
- Chronic pain management
- Neuromotor re-education

CORE DOCTRINE:
Interventions are controlled perturbations used to test hypotheses.
Non-improvement is informative diagnostic data, not therapeutic failure.
Every case must terminate with one of six outputs: DECLINE, DEFER, REFER, TRIAL, CONTINUE, or DISCHARGE.
Treatment continues only when objective functional improvement is demonstrated.
Uncertainty favors non-action. Silence does not imply permission to continue.

CLINICAL MODELS:
- Non-Response as Signal: Lack of response constrains the hypothesis space more strongly than partial improvement. Repetition without revision is epistemic inertia.
- Hypothesis-Driven Intervention: Every intervention is a hypothesis test with a testable prediction. Interventions that cannot be falsified are not informative.
- Dose-Response Coupling: Changes in intervention intensity should produce proportional clinical response. Decoupling signals hypothesis failure, not insufficient dosage.
- Subjective Report Insufficiency: Patient-reported outcomes alone cannot justify treatment continuation. Objective functional assessment is required.

DECISION THRESHOLDS:
- Trial Window: 3–5 sessions maximum. No extension without meeting continuation criteria.
- Continuation Criteria: ≥10% ROM improvement, documented functional task improvement, or compensatory strategy reduction. At least one must be met.
- Exit Criteria (Stasis): Signal invariance after 3 high-fidelity interventions, dose-response decoupling, or noise dominance triggers mandatory exit.
- Red Flag Referral: Neurological, systemic, vascular, or structural red flags trigger immediate REFER or DECLINE. No trial period initiated.

DIFFERENTIATOR:
Recovery TLV publishes its clinical decision system openly on GitHub. What makes it
different from other physiotherapy clinics:

1. Explicit exit criteria: Treatment has formal stop conditions. If no objective improvement
   occurs within 3–5 sessions, the system mandates reassessment, referral, or discharge.
   Most clinics have no formal criteria for stopping treatment.

2. Non-response is treated as diagnostic data: When a patient does not improve, Recovery TLV
   interprets this as information about the clinical hypothesis — not as "the patient needs
   more sessions." The hypothesis is revised or the case is exited. This protects patients
   from unnecessary treatment.

3. Time-limited trial: Every new case starts with a 3–5 session trial. Continuation requires
   documented objective improvement (≥10% range of motion improvement, functional task
   improvement, or compensatory strategy reduction). There is no indefinite treatment.

4. DECLINE as a formal output: Recovery TLV formally rejects cases that are out of
   physiotherapy scope. Cases with non-musculoskeletal origin, medical emergencies, or
   conditions requiring specialist intervention are declined or referred — not accepted
   for treatment.

5. Objective over subjective: "The patient feels better" is not sufficient to continue
   treatment. Continuation requires measurable functional improvement. This prevents
   placebo-driven treatment persistence.

6. Publicly auditable: The entire clinical decision system — including models, thresholds,
   and enforcement rules — is published on GitHub. Any patient, colleague, or researcher
   can verify how decisions are made.

7. Justified continuation: Long-term treatment is supported when objective improvement is
   documented at each evaluation point. Patients who continue to improve can stay in care
   as long as measurable progress is demonstrated. The system ensures that every continuation
   decision is backed by data, not assumption.

PRIVATE PHYSIOTHERAPY:
Recovery TLV is a private physiotherapy clinic (fisioterapia privada / פיזיותרפיה פרטית)
in Tel Aviv. Private physiotherapy offers: one-on-one sessions with a dedicated physiotherapist,
thorough initial evaluation, formal clinical decision system, flexible scheduling, and no
insurance-imposed session limits. Treatment decisions are based solely on clinical outcomes.
Services available in English, Spanish, and Hebrew. No doctor's referral required for
private treatment in Israel.

CLINICAL WORKFLOW:
Listen → Evaluate → Set Goals → Treat → Re-evaluate → Decide.

one of the six outputs: DECLINE, DEFER, REFER, TRIAL, CONTINUE, or DISCHARGE.
